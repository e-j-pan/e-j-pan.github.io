Walnut Almond Chia Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup raw walnuts
  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1-2 dates or 1 tsp maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract or cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups filtered water

Instructions

1

Pre-soak nuts (optional but recommended)

Soak the walnuts and almonds in water for 4-8 hours or overnight in the refrigerator. This softens them and makes a creamier milk. Drain and rinse thoroughly before using.

2

Prepare the Nutr machine

Make sure your Nutr machine is clean and plugged in. Add the soaked and rinsed walnuts and almonds to the Nutr chamber. Add the chia seeds. Add any optional sweeteners or flavorings. Add the filtered water to the fill line (approximately 1 1/2 cups).

3

Select program and blend

Close the lid securely. Press the power button to turn on the machine. Choose the "Nuts" program (typically by pressing the program button until you reach the nut setting). Press start and let the machine complete its cycle (approximately 5-7 minutes).

4

Strain if desired

Your milk is ready to use directly from the machine. For an extra-smooth texture, you can strain it through a nut milk bag or fine mesh strainer.

5

Storage

Transfer to a sealed container. Refrigerate and use within 3-5 days. Shake well before each use as separation is natural.

Tips

For a thicker consistency, add 1-2 more tablespoons of chia seeds and let the mixture sit for 10 minutes after blending. This creates a delicious pudding-like texture! For additional flavor variations, try adding a dash of cardamom, nutmeg, or cocoa powder.
